Scaling BlockP to 3,000 Daily Clicks

How I grew a recovery platform from zero to 400K+ organic clicks in 18 months through topical authority and a strategic domain migration.

0 Daily Organic Clicks

What I Had

A powerful product solving a real problem (digital self-control), but limited organic visibility and growth structure.

  • ✖ 0 organic traffic at launch (June 2024)
  • ✖ ~300 daily downloads with no strong SEO support
  • ✖ No structured SEO content strategy
  • ✖ No topical authority in search engines
  • ✖ Limited keyword coverage
  • ✖ No multilingual SEO presence
  • ✖ Weak internal linking structure
  • ✖ No global domain positioning (.in for Tier-1)

Despite having a strong product, search engines were not fully understanding or ranking BlockP effectively.

What I Did

1. Built a Strong SEO Foundation

Keyword Strategy (High-Intent Focus)

Targeted solution-seeking keywords (porn blocker, how to quit porn, NoFap benefits).

👉 Focus: intent-driven traffic, not just volume

Topical Authority System

Built deep core clusters (Recovery, NoFap, Sexual Health) to rank for hundreds of related keywords.

Content Optimization

SEO-optimized H1-H3, featured snippet targeting, high CTR titles, and KISS writing.

2. Multilingual SEO Expansion

Translated high-performing pages to capture international traffic beyond English.

👉 Result: Expanded global reach and impressions

3. Technical SEO Optimization

Improved speed, architecture, schema, and mobile UX for better crawlability.

👉 Result: Better indexing and ranking efficiency

4. Domain Strategy Shift (.in → .io)

Migrated to BlockP.io (June 2025) with SEO-safe redirects to close the Tier-1 trust gap.

👉 Result: Improved global trust & growth scalability

5. Internal Linking & Structure

Connected blogs to feature pages, reduced orphan pages, and improved crawl depth.
