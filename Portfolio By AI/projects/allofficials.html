What I Had

Let’s be blunt — the Allofficials site had potential, but the execution was weak. They averaged around 1,000 organic visitors a month, but it was low-quality, scattered, non-transactional traffic.

  • ✖ Zero Keyword Focus: No ranking visibility on actual money keywords. Core terms like "compression t-shirt" and "ribbed vest" were dead stuck at positions 50–60.
